About Me

I'm Jaber Ahmed, a web developer and motion designer with a focus on clean interfaces and cinematic video editing. Over the years I’ve built websites, dashboards and live score systems, produced motion graphics and edited raw footage into engaging content for social and streaming platforms. My approach combines technical front-end skills (HTML/CSS/JS/Tailwind) with visual storytelling — ensuring both polished visuals and fast, accessible experiences.

  • • Front-end development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind
  • • Motion & editing: After Effects, Premiere Pro, camera-to-edit workflows
  • • Data visualization & dashboards for small businesses
  • • Available for freelance projects, collaborations and consulting
